A closer look at the Otaki River Floodplain

This image shows the wide floodplain of the Otaki River and hence it's ability to migrate across the floodplain. Geological controls on the Otaki are as follows below. The floodplain is made of alluvium deposits with shifting sand dunes on the front by the Tasman Sea. Towards the west lies the Tararua ranges made of greywache rock
